Library

Our state-of-the-art library is a treasure trove of knowledge, housing over 50,000 books covering various subjects and disciplines. The library provides a serene environment for students to study and research.

  • 50,000+ books and reference materials
  • Digital library with e-books and online journals
  • Reading halls with comfortable seating
  • Computer terminals for research
  • Photocopying and printing facilities
  • Quiet study zones and group discussion areas
  • Open access system for easy book retrieval
  • Librarian assistance for research support

Hostel

Separate hostels for boys and girls provide a safe, comfortable, and conducive environment for outstation students.

Boys Hostel

  • Capacity: 200 students
  • Single and double occupancy rooms
  • 24/7 security and warden supervision
  • Common room with TV and games
  • Mess facility with nutritious meals

Girls Hostel

  • Capacity: 150 students
  • Well-furnished rooms with attached bathrooms
  • Strict security measures
  • Study halls and recreation areas
  • Hygienic mess with vegetarian meals

Both hostels have Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, medical assistance, and round-the-clock power backup.

Transportation

Our college operates a fleet of buses covering all major routes in the city and surrounding areas, ensuring safe and convenient transportation for students.

  • Fleet of 15 buses with GPS tracking
  • Routes covering all major areas of the city
  • Experienced and trained drivers
  • Regular maintenance and safety checks
  • Affordable transportation fees
  • Separate buses for different routes
  • Punctual service with fixed timings
  • Female conductors on all buses

Bus Routes: North Zone, South Zone, East Zone, West Zone, Central Zone

Timings: Morning pickup: 7:00 AM - 8:30 AM | Evening drop: 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM
