Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate Courses

  • Completed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved categories)
  • Subject-specific requirements as per course selection
  • Valid entrance exam scores (if applicable)

Postgraduate Courses

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field from recognized university
  • Minimum 55% aggregate marks (50% for reserved categories)
  • Entrance exam qualification (if required)
  • Interview/Group Discussion (for selected courses)

Professional Courses

  • 10+2 with minimum 60% marks
  • Relevant entrance exam scores
  • Personal interview and aptitude test
  • Additional requirements as per specific course
