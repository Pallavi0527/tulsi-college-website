Our History

Deogiri Pratishthan Sanchalit Tulsi Computer Science And IT College, Beed is a leading IT educating minority institute affiliated to Dr.Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad started in 2006 & declared as a minority institute in 2012 . College is situated in the center of Beed city at Adarsh Nagar with campus area 668 sq. /m. We are also having our own building near Adarsh Nagar behind govt ITI in which we are running a BSc general course. In the second building the college is having three classrooms and four labs.

This campus is near to main campus. Our college is mono-faculty i.e. science faculty program for UG like BCS, BCA, BSc & PG like , MSc computer & MMS are effectively run by our college . Nearly 670 students from Beed and nearby villages are taking education in our college. Our college provides value added courses like communication skill etc, short term courses like MSCIT, DTP etc. . Number of our alumni students are performing their best in various careers or businesses. While keeping vision and mission in our mind, the college tries to develop good citizens with good moral values.

Today, Tulsi College stands as a beacon of educational excellence, having produced thousands of successful alumni who are making significant contributions in their respective fields across India and abroad.
